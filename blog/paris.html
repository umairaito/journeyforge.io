Best Time to Visit Paris

Spring (April-June) and Fall (September-November) are the ideal times to visit Paris. The weather is pleasant, tourist crowds are manageable, and the city displays seasonal beauty.

  • Spring: Cherry blossoms, tulips in Tuileries Garden, mild temperatures
  • Summer: Long days, festivals, but more tourists and higher prices
  • Fall: Golden leaves, harvest season, comfortable weather
  • Winter: Christmas markets, fewer crowds, magical holiday lights
