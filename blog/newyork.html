Best Time to Visit New York

Spring (April-June) and Fall (September-November) are the best times to visit NYC. The weather is pleasant, and the city hosts major events.

  • Spring: Cherry blossoms in Brooklyn, pleasant temperatures
  • Summer: Hot and humid, but free events and rooftop bars
  • Fall: Stunning foliage in Central Park, comfortable weather
  • Winter: Christmas magic, ice skating, but cold temperatures
